Our Clear Vision

From the beginning, Open Mushaf was built around core principles focused on usability, authenticity, and accessibility

Usability

Natural gesture-based navigation and intuitive design that feels effortless for every user

Authenticity

Multiple authentic recitations (Riwayat) with color-coded Tajweed rules for proper pronunciation

Accessibility

Works on all platforms, fully offline, with personalized settings that remember your preferences

Offline First

No internet? No problem. The entire Quran, recitations, and Tafseer remain accessible offline

Rich Content

Access to multiple Tafseer sources and reading tracking to support your spiritual journey

Multi-Platform

Seamless performance on Android, iOS, and web with native features on each platform
